About Us

We are Team SHEAA, comprised of four Villanova Computer Engineering students, Satrant Bains, Liam Baker, Kieran Seven, and Alex Fink. We have been selected by our faculty advisor, Dr. Xun Jiao, to participate in his project idea that is funded through Villanova and L3Harris' partnered program called INNOVATE. Our goal in this project is to simulate the errors that can occur in the hardware that AI models, primarily NLP models, are run on. Through this error simulation, we hope to explore the robustness of these NLP models, and gain insight into the limits that this area of AI has.

INNOVATE: The L3Harris Summer Program with Villanova

INNOVATE is a partnership program with Villanova University's College of Engineering and L3Harris Technologies. Over the course of two-months, student teams complete a functional prototype, which can be physical or digital, under the guidance of a faculty mentor. The students can come up with the project idea themselves, or the faculty advisor can propose a project idea and search for capable students to carry out the project. The latter case is the situation that our team has pursued with Dr. Xun Jiao. L3Harris was kind enough to fund this project, giving each team a budget of $3500 and covering the expenses for housing and meal plans on campus for the two month summer program.
