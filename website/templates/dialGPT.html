Uses

Chatbots: DialoGPT can be used to create chatbot systems that engage in text-based conversations with users. It generates responses based on the given context and can handle multi-turn conversations.

Virtual Assistants: DialoGPT can serve as the core component of virtual assistants or voice-activated systems, providing natural and interactive responses to user queries and commands.

Customer Support: DialoGPT can be employed in customer support systems to automate responses to frequently asked questions or assist human agents by providing suggestions or answers based on the context of the customer's query.

Language Tutoring: DialoGPT can be used in educational applications as a language tutor, providing interactive conversational practice and assistance in language learning.

Creative Writing and Story Generation: DialoGPT can generate text in a conversational manner, making it suitable for creative writing, story generation, or interactive storytelling applications.

Training

The model is trained on 147M multi-turn dialogue from Reddit discussion threads dating from 2005-2017.
